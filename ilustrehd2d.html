Ilustre-Project HD2D

Platforms: Windows
Game Engine: Unreal Engine 5
Team Size: 4
Role: Programmer and Music composer.

Synopsis

Ilustre is a cooperative multiplayer game in which at the beginning of the game you can choose to be a warrior, an archer, a wizard or a guardian.
Each class has different skills that will help you solve different puzzles found in the world, so you will have to get together with your friends and coordinate to overcome the challenges of this game.
