TOWER DEFENSE PROJECT

Platforms: PC
Game Engine: Unreal Engine 5
Team Size: 1
Non commercial Project
Synopsis: Features:
In this project you have to set up camps that will generate units to wipe out enemy units.
There are several types of units, such as soldiers, medics, tanks, miners and planes.
There are also different types of terrain, some of which will make some units go slower or faster and some of which will not be traversable.
The money used to buy camps will regenerate over time but the more you spend the longer it takes to regenerate.
  • Variety of units
  • Scalability
  • Different types of terrain
  • Different attributes for each unit depending on the terrain
