LIMITLESS DUNGEON

Platforms: Android
Game Engine: Unity
Team Size: 1
Non commercial Project
Synopsis:
In Limitless Dungeon the player progresses through different floors of a randomly generated dungeon.
In this dungeon there are enemies and stairs that allow the player to advance to the next floor.
The number of floors the player manages to overcome will be the score of the game.
Features:
  • Health bar
  • Rolls
  • Stamina bar
  • Leaderboard
  • Register and Login System
